Core Services

🔹 Advisory on Share & Asset Recovery

We provide structured advisory to individuals and families seeking to trace and recover unclaimed, lost, or long-forgotten financial assets, including shares and dividends. Our role is limited to guidance, case evaluation, and outlining the procedural roadmap for recovery.

🔹 Assistance in IEPF Claim Processing

We assist clients in understanding and navigating the process of claims with the IEPF Authority, including documentation guidance, procedural requirements, and authorised representation where applicable.

🔹 Documentation Support

We assist clients in identifying, organising, and preparing documentation required for share recovery processes, including affidavits, indemnities, declarations, and supporting records.

🔹 Coordination with Companies & RTAs

Procedural coordination with companies and Registrar & Transfer Agents including Link Intime and KFin.

🔹 Share Recovery & Regularisation

Assistance in recovery and regularisation of holdings where records or certificates are unavailable.

🔹 Transmission & Succession

Support in understanding transmission procedures and legal heir documentation requirements.

🔹 Dematerialisation Support

Assistance in converting physical shareholdings into electronic demat format.

🔹 KYC Alignment & Corrections

Resolving discrepancies in signatures, names, and KYC records affecting recovery processes.

🔹 Case Assessment & Feasibility

Initial case evaluation to determine documentation requirements and procedural feasibility.
